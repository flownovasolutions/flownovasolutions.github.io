A note on architecture

FlowNova recommends the simplest architecture that solves the problem. A single app with many screens is often the right call. We recommend splitting into multiple connected apps only when different user groups need fundamentally different experiences, performance thresholds require it, or separate security boundaries are involved.

This is an architecture decision we make together during discovery — it never changes your price tier. Complexity drives the tier, not artifact count.

How pricing works

Complexity Drives the Tier

FlowNova prices by the complexity of the problem we solve — not by the number of apps, screens, or flows we deliver. Pricing is structured around the depth of your data architecture, the sophistication of your integrations, the reach across your organization, and the level of governance required. No surprises, just intentional, engineered work.
