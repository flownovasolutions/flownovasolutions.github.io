The Daily Reality of Oil & Gas Operations

AFEs routed through email chains that take days to approve. Field inspections filled out on paper and re-entered into a spreadsheet back at the office. Production data hand-keyed from one system to another. Change orders tracked in someone's inbox. Compliance reports assembled manually every month from five different sources.

These aren't edge cases — they're standard operating procedure across Oklahoma's oil and gas industry. And every one of them is costing you time, accuracy, and money. FlowNova fixes that — not by ripping out your systems, but by building the operational layer between them that never existed.

What we solve

Built for the Way Oil & Gas Actually Operates

AFE & Purchase Order Workflows

Multi-level approval chains automated with role-based routing, threshold logic, and real-time status tracking. No more chasing signatures through email — every AFE and PO moves through a structured workflow with full audit trail and automatic notifications at every stage.

Mobile Field Inspection & Data Capture

Purpose-built apps that work on any device — even with limited connectivity. Field teams capture inspection data, photos, and readings directly into structured forms that sync to your central database. No more paper forms, no more re-entry, no more lost data.

Compliance & Safety Reporting

OCC, EPA, OSHA — the reporting never stops. Automated compliance workflows that pull data from your operational systems, flag gaps before they become violations, and generate audit-ready reports without the monthly scramble.

Production & Operations Visibility

Real-time dashboards that consolidate production data, well status, equipment performance, and operational KPIs into a single view. Stop asking "where are we on that?" and start making decisions from actual data — updated automatically, accessible anywhere.

Contractor & Vendor Management

Contractor onboarding, insurance tracking, work order automation, and invoice processing — all in one system. Every vendor interaction documented, every requirement tracked, every payment tied to verified deliverables.
